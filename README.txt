aaolm.portlet.facebook

Introduction
============

Facebook Fan Page Portlet for Advanced Aquarist

This product installs a very simple portlet for rendering a Facebook Fan Page
widget in a portlet in Plone.  After installation, simply add the portlet 
where you would like it to show up in your Plone portal.  Then paste your
Facebook Fans widget javascript into the text area and click Save.
